Grandma's Secret Apple pie

This apple pie recipe has been in my family for generations and never fails to delight

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6 medium size apples,peeled and sliced

Instructions

  1. preheat oven to 375f(190c)
  2. in abowl mix the suger and cinnamon
  3. toss the apples in the suger-cinnamon mixture
